#this is a small project i've been working on if you are like me, you hate having a lot of pdf/docx on your desktop and you just wanna clean those as fast as possible, well this program is for you. Easy to use:

0- IMPORTANT !!!!!!!!RUN AS ADMINistrator
1- Press fetch files button
2-select the files you want to gather in the same folder, move them to the right using ">>" button
3- arrange and choose the name of the folder
4-repeat 3 until satisfied or no files are detected on your desktop (you can gather all files in one folder too, just do step 2 and 3 to all the files.
5- Important! press "finish" button to save your arranged files.
6- "Reset" button is so you can start over, ONLY WORKS BEFORE PRESSING FINISH
